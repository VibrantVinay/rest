Our Heritage

A Story Steeped

The Origin

From Estate
to Cup

The Varikkans Tea Company was born from an obsession: the belief that tea ‚Äî when grown with care, harvested at the right moment, and blended with artistry ‚Äî can tell the story of its land as powerfully as the finest wine.

Founded in 2026 by the Varikkan family in Kozhikode, Kerala, our journey began with a single estate and a handwritten recipe book passed through four generations. Today, we source from 12 partner estates across South India, Sri Lanka, and Darjeeling.

Our kitchen transforms these leaves into full culinary experiences ‚Äî teas that are brewed, cooked into sauces, smoked over, and infused into every corner of our menu.

2026
The First Cup

Founding of Varikkans Tea Company in the Malabar coastal quarter of Kozhikode with a 30-seat tearoom.

2027
Estate Partnerships

Formal agreements signed with six estates across Nilgiri and Wayanad. First limited-edition single-origin release.

2029
National Recognition

Featured in Forbes India's 'Most Distinctive Dining Experiences' and awarded Best Artisanal Tearoom, South India.

2031
International Expansion

Opening of Varikkans London and Varikkans Dubai, bringing Malabar hospitality to a global stage.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01
Provenance

We trace every ingredient to its source. Our guests deserve to know the story behind their cup and their plate. Transparency is not a policy ‚Äî it is a promise.

02
Craftsmanship

In an age of automation, we choose the slow path. Hand-plucking, traditional rolling, clay-pot brewing ‚Äî the methods that take longer are the ones worth keeping.

03
Stewardship

The land that feeds us is a responsibility, not a resource. We work exclusively with certified organic estates and donate 2% of revenue to rural farmers' welfare.

04
Community

We employ exclusively from within 50km of each location, train baristas in traditional blending arts, and host monthly free tea education sessions for the public.

05
Innovation

Our R&D kitchen experiments with tea-infused fermentation, novel pairing science, and collaborative research with Kerala Agricultural University.

06
Hospitality

In Malayalam, we say atithi devo bhava ‚Äî the guest is god. Every reservation, every interaction, every cup is a sacred act of welcome.
