Our Story

Started in 2026, Varikkans Tea Stall began with a simple mission: to bring the farm-to-table experience to the heart of the city.

Our executive chef, Elena Rostova, believes that food is an emotional experience. We source our vegetables from local community gardens and our spices directly from artisan growers.
